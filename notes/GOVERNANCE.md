# Governance & Governance Policy

## 1. The Immutable Layer (Hard-Coded)
The following are read-only for all agents to ensure stability:
* **Infrastructure Specs:** Static IP addresses, Port numbers, and Hardware MAC addresses.
* **Safety Protocol:** "Never delete files without a backup," "Never execute `sudo` without Boss approval."
* **System Identity:** The core definition of the "War Room" itself.

## 2. The Feedback & Promotion Loop
* **Direct Feedback:** When a Boss gives feedback in a One-on-One, the agent logs this as a "Boss Feedback" entry in their journal.
* **Skill Promotion:** Agents can propose updates to their `skills.md`. These must be approved by the Boss in Dev Mode before becoming part of the verified "Standard Operating Procedures."
